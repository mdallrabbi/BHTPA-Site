SHSTP Job Fair-2017

Venue: SHSTP, Jessore

October-5, 2017

Open For All. No Registration Required.

SHSTP Job Fair-2017

Are you looking for a job? Are you tired of sending resumes left and right and never getting an answer? Are you frustrated that all Job Fairs are happening in Dhaka?

If you are going visit our Job Fair 2017. You will have the opportunity to meet employers and to interview for several positions during the course of the day, meeting the people who make the hiring decisions. You will save a lot of time since there will be no need to craft a customized personal statement for each company you visit: You’ll be able to tell them why you wish to work for them, and you’ll have the chance to sell yourself.

There are also couple of seminars to let you know the future of IT industries, your opportunities there, the skill you need and a workshop to help you finalize your Resume!

Besides you will have the opportunity to visit the biggest Software Technology Park so far, the Sheikh Hasina Software Technology Park, Jessore.

Welcome Message

Message from Honourable State Minister
Zunaid Ahmed Palak, MP
State Minister of Post, Telecommunications & Information Technology

It’s absolutely incredible to finally able to say that the Sheikh Hasina Software Technology Park (SHSTP) in Jessore is an idea that has seen the light of day. Seeing that the work is almost done and a companies being allocated here is a source of immense joy for the people who worked tirelessly into planning and executing this project. Their efforts have truly come to fruition. I recalled the day when our honourable Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina announced to build this park, a long cherished dream of the people of 10 southern district of Bangladesh on 27 December 2010. Since then, we worked hard to make the dream a reality.
Now, the SHSTP is in place and it is our duties to make it a vibrant place for activities including but not limited to software development, Business Process outsourcing, call Centre, robotics etc. To start with, I am happy that the Bangladesh hiTech Park Authority is organizing a Job Fair over there. We expected that more than 20 employers within and outside of Jessore area will join the fair. Besides there will be several seminars, workshop, round table discussion, panel discussion etc to give an overall idea of the current and future IT industries opportunities for the job seekers. I hope, these entire endeavors will help our youth to shape their future as well as stimulate the future of Bangladesh. I would like to welcome you all in SHSTP Job Fair 2017.
Please do come.
Joy Bangla
Joy Bangabandhu

Message from Managing Director
Hosne Ara Begum ndc
Managing Director (Secretary), Bangladesh Hi Tech Park Authority

Welcome to the first ever Job Fair organize at the Sheikh Hasina S oftware Technology Park (SHSTP), Jessore, a bold step towards our long cherished goal of digital Bangladesh.
To provide world class infrastructure and incentive facilities to the entrepreneurs, investors from home and abroad the SHSTP comprises a Multi Tenant Building with 232,000 sq. ft area, a 12 stories dormitory with 3-star facilities, Cafeteria, Amphitheater etc.
So far, 34 companies have been allocated space in this park and more are in pipe line. Few of them have already started their operations.

The multi fold Job Fair will help both employer and job seekers to know each others requirements, establish a direct network among them. The fair will also help the others to explore the facilities we are providing in SHSTP.
The Job Fair is also an unique opportunities for the students who wants to pursue their career in ICT Sectors. There will be spectacular seminars, workshop for them which will not only portrait of future career but also help them to understand the requirement to grab the opportunities.
On behalf of Bangladesh Hi Tech Park Authority, I would like to welcome you all once again to our Career Fair 2017.
